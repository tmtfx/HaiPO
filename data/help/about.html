About me and eulogies


Here we are, just me and you. I'm a passionate Haiku user, who fell in love with BeOS 5 in the 2000's. I'm interested in software translations to my local language (Friulian). And write some python apps for Haiku using its API. I'm happy to collaborate on the Haiku-PyAPI project and I give a heartfelt thank you to coolcoder613eb and Zardshard for giving life to this project and for continuing to support it, resolving its challenging issues. If you want to thank me with a small donation you can buy me a coffee at my Ko-Fi page. I hope this program proves to be useful to you. For any issue open a ticket on HaiPO github page
